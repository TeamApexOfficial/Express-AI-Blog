Express AI

What is Express AI?

Express AI is a browser extension designed to enhance web accessibility through AI-driven text-to-speech technology. Unlike traditional screen readers, Express AI aims to capture tone, emotion, and intent to provide a more engaging and human-like listening experience.

To improve the experience for visually impaired individuals, we (Team Apex) have developed a solution in the form of a browser extension called "Express AI." This extension aims to go beyond simply reading the text on web pages. While existing tools can read aloud the content, they do not capture or convey the emotions or tone behind the words. Our goal is to address this gap by incorporating emotional intelligence into the reading experience. "Express AI" uses advanced AI models to analyse the emotional context of the text and adjust the voice output accordingly. This means that when a web page is read aloud, the extension will not just deliver the text in a neutral tone but will also adapt the emotional tone to match the writer's intent. For example, a sentence expressing excitement will be read with a lively tone, while a more serious or sober statement will be delivered in a thoughtful manner. By adding emotional tone and sound effects, "Express AI" helps users connect with the writer's emotions and feelings, providing a more engaging and meaningful experience. This feature enhances the way visually impaired users interact with online content, allowing them to better understand the nuances and intent behind the text. Additionally, "Express AI" also benefits sighted users who wish to listen to any web page. Whether for convenience or simply to enjoy a more dynamic listening experience, the extension provides a richer and more immersive way to engage with content. Ultimately, our aim is to create a more inclusive and dynamic browsing experience for all users, improving the way everyone interacts with online material.
