Express AI

What is Express AI?

Express AI is a browser extension designed to enhance web accessibility through AI-driven text-to-speech technology. Unlike traditional screen readers, Express AI aims to capture tone, emotion, and intent to provide a more engaging and human-like listening experience.

Advantages of Express AI

  • ✔ AI-Powered Voice – Reads text with natural and human-like speech.
  • ✔ Emotion Detection (Coming Soon) – Understands tone and adapts its voice.
  • ✔ Easy to Use – One-click activation with a simple UI.
  • ✔ Works in Real-Time – Reads live webpages instantly.
  • ✔ Optimized for Accessibility – Helps visually impaired users navigate content.
